# .impeccable.md

## Design Context

### Users
Financial analysts, fund managers, and technology investors attending TrendForce's New York seminar. They consume this page on desktop in a professional context — typically at a desk, making a deliberate decision about whether to register. A smaller portion will land on mobile via email or social links.

### Brand Personality
**Authoritative · Editorial · Precise**
TrendForce is a research firm — the tone should feel like a premium industry publication, not an event marketing site. Confidence, not hype. The interface should feel like something a serious analyst trusts.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark. This is an evening professional event; the dark treatment matches the gravitas.
- **Accent**: Warm gold (`#c9a96e`) — evokes financial data, premium credentials.
- **Typographic voice**: Editorial — large serif display headings contrast with tight grotesque body text.
- **References**: aanstekelijk.nl, osmo.supply, flowfest.co.uk — bold, non-template, motion-forward.
- **Anti-reference**: No conference-template look (hero banner + colored boxes + stock photos).

### Design Principles
1. **Motion earns its place** — every animation either directs attention or confirms an action. No decoration for its own sake.
2. **Hierarchy through contrast** — large vs small, serif vs grotesque, bright vs muted. Never four sizes that are 1.1× apart.
3. **The page is a publication** — treat each section like a spread. Generous whitespace, editorial rhythm.
4. **Performance first** — GPU-only transforms, no layout thrashing. Smooth at 60fps on a phone.
5. **Respect the reader** — no autoplay video, no scroll-jacking, no forced wait states. The user is always in control.
